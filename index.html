JAP'AFRICA DINING

Our Story

Where two worlds
meet on one plate.

The expression of a private chef driven by creativity, culture, and precision.

Founded by Murphy Mouanga, our mission is to blend the precision, harmony, and artistry of Japanese cuisine with the bold, soulful flavours of Africa — a fusion we call Jap'Africa.

Each experience is intentionally intimate — designed not only to satisfy, but to evoke emotion through flavour, texture, and presentation.

This is not just food. It is a story told through every course.

Meet Chef Murphy
The Chef

A vision of
culture & precision

Rooted in African heritage and inspired by Japanese culinary discipline — Jap'Africa was born to bring two worlds together on one plate.

Murphy's culinary journey began with a deep appreciation for Japanese technique, paired with the rich, expressive flavours of his African heritage. He discovered that food could be a bridge connecting traditions, sharing stories, and bringing people together.

Every dish is crafted with intention, creativity, and respect for both worlds — where Japanese elegance meets African warmth in every bite.
